About me!

I love to listen to a good story, and sometimes not so good ones, but one of the most important values for me since I was a kid was listening to others. I'm passionate about UX because as a designer I can do that, listen and understand humans! and I can improve peoples' lives through products (digital or tangibles). It can't get better than that!
‍
I live in Raleigh, North Carolina with my three favorite people in my world; my husband and two daughters. When I'm not with them or studying, I'm in the swimming pool, adding up some miles for my open waters.
